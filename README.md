# The Problem

Every year millions of students preparing for:

* JEE
* NEET
* UPSC
* GATE
* CAT
* Semester Exams
* Placement Season

face:

* Academic burnout
* Anxiety
* Sleep deprivation
* Loss of motivation
* Emotional exhaustion
* Declining performance

The problem?

Most students only realize something is wrong when burnout has already started affecting their performance.

---

# What is Himmat?

Himmat is an AI-powered student wellness platform that predicts burnout before it happens.

Instead of simply asking:

> "How are you feeling today?"

Himmat continuously analyzes:

```text
Sleep
+
Study Hours
+
Mood
+
Journal Entries
+
Exam Pressure
+
Screen Time
+
Performance Trends
```

to generate:

```text
Burnout Risk
Recovery Plans
Stress Insights
Exam Readiness
```

---

# Impact

Himmat is not designed to replace counselors, therapists, or mental health professionals.

Its purpose is to help students:

* Recognize burnout early
* Build healthier study habits
* Improve self-awareness
* Stay consistent during preparation
* Seek support before reaching a crisis point
